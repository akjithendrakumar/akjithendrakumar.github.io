# Career paths: platform engineering and product thinking

May 25, 2026

Platform engineering is not only about tools and infrastructure. It also benefits from product thinking.

The more the platform is treated like an internal product, the more likely it is to reduce friction, improve developer experience, and solve the right problems for the teams using it.
