# Career paths: platform engineering and product thinking

May 25, 2026

Platform engineering sits at the intersection of infrastructure, developer experience, and business outcomes. Success requires both technical depth and product thinking.

Advice:

- Work backwards from developer experience: reduce friction and make the common path easy and fast.
- Measure outcomes: deployments per day, lead time, and mean time to recovery — use metrics to prioritize work.
- Communicate value: translate platform work into business impact (cost savings, faster delivery, improved reliability).

Final note: treat platforms as products and customers (developers) as users — iterate, measure, and improve.
